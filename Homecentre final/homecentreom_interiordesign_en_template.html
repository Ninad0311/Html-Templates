Our design experts will meet with you. They will be able to give you insights and a comprehensive overview of the project that will enable you to make informed decisions, best suited to your requirements. Bring any photos or ideas you have and we'll work with you and create a digital mood board.

Modular Wardrobe

What is modular furniture?

Modular furniture is furniture that is customised to suit your requirements. They are manufactured in factories using advanced technology, generally made in engineered wood. Modular furniture is easy to assemble and comes with a great finish.

What modular units can you make?

We offer modular systems, wardrobes, sliding and hanging doors, children’s storage, living room storage and sofas.

Will the modular furniture be made on site?

All our modular furniture is entirely factory-made and finished. The furniture is delivered to your doorstep in flat-pack boxes. Only the assembly and installation are done on site.

How is your modular furniture transported?

Our fleet of vehicles will deliver the modular furniture to your home, directly from the warehouse.

What is the warranty on modular furniture?

Modular furniture comes with a minimum warranty of 2 years against manufacturing defects.

What is covered in the warranty?

The warranty covers manufacturing defects in the wood work.

Who will carry out the installation/assembly?

We have a team of trained techicians who will assemble and install your modular furniture.

Who do I contact in case I have an issue with my modular furniture after installation?

Please contact Customer Support to report any issues that arise after installation. You can also get in touch with your Interior design consultant.

Interior Design & Build

Can you renovate my existing home?

No, the premises must be prepared for installation from Home Centre. We don’t remove or shift existing furniture.

Can I procure the material myself through my own set of vendors?

We offer existing lines and collections available at Home Centre. We do not offer bespoke or custom-made furniture and accessories.

What qualifications do your Interior designers have?

All our designers are qualified interior designers from premier institutes, who have a proven track record with reputed companies.

What can I expect from the Interior designers?

Our interior designer will meet with you to discuss and understand your requirements and budget, conduct a site visit, prepare two proposals and guide you through the purchase process. They will be present on-site at the time of delivery to ensure accuracy and full completion as per agreed design concept.

Can I suggest changes in the design after the order is confirmed?

Once the order is confirmed, you will not be able to change the requirements. However, your design consultant will be able to guide you better on this.

Will I get regular progress updates?

Once the project is initiated, we’ll share a project plan with you, followed by regular progress updates.

Who will do the installation for my project?

Furniture and home décor will be delivered and installed by our team. However, installation of pendant lamps/ chandeliers, wall mirrors and wall art are not done by us. An external third party can be suggested for execution under the supervision of our interior designer. Home Centre will not be responsible for quotations or pricing.

How long does it take to complete the designs?

Initial designs will be ready for review 7 days after our designers visit your site.
