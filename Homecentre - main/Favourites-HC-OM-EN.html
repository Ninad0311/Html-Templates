So how do you favourite a product?

If you’ve fallen in love with one of our products, but can’t decide whether to pick it up right away, no worries. You can now add it to your ‘Favourites’ and buy it later at your convenience.

Here’s how:

1. Browse through our products.

You’ll see a grey heart-shaped symbol on the top left-hand corner of every product image. This is the ‘Favourites’ icon.

2. Click on the Favourites icon.

When you favourite a product you like, the heart-shaped icon will turn red and the product will be added to your Favourites section. Just remember to sign in first.

3. Buy it later from your Favourites page.

When you come back to buy that great product, head to the Favourites section under My Account and add it directly to your basket to purchase it.

It’s as simple as that! Go on, save your favourites today.
