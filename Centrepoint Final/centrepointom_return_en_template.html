Returns

Here's how the returns process works.

Returns should be easy. We couldn’t agree more.

Let's show you how to return your products in three easy ways.
There are some products we don't take back. Check out our Returns Policy for more details.

1. Return to store

1. Fill out your return slip

This will be emailed to you after your products are delivered.

2. Visit your nearest store

Wrap your household products in their original packaging and bring them over with your return slip and invoice to any Centrepoint store.

3. Hand over your return

An authorised staff member will take your package and hand you an acknowledgement receipt.

4. Receive your refund

This will be processed after we check your returns. If you’ve paid by card, you can receive a refund to your card or opt for a Credit Note. If you’ve paid by cash, you can ask for a cash refund, Credit Note or a refund to My Credit in your account.

2. Request a return online

 

1. Request your return

Go to Order History in My Account, select the products you want to return and place a return request.

2. Box up your products

Wrap and seal your products in their original packaging or call us at 800-7-6927 if you no longer have it.

3. Hand over your return

Our courier partner may take 3-5 business days to pick it up. They’ll give you an acknowledgement receipt when they do.

4. Receive your refund

We’ll check your returns and credit your refund to your card in 4-15 days. If you’ve paid by cash, you’ll receive a refund to My Credit in your account. Guest users will receive a Return Voucher.

3. Call 800-7-6927 to schedule a pick-up 

 

1. Call for a pick up

Speak to us at 800-7-6927 to schedule a pick-up time and location. Make sure you fill out the return slip first.

2. Box up your products

Wrap and seal your products in their original packaging or call us if you no longer have it.

3. Hand over your return

Our courier partner may take 3-5 business days to pick it up. They’ll give you an acknowledgement receipt when they do.

4. Receive your refund

We’ll check your returns and credit your refund to your card within 4-15 days. If you’ve paid by cash, you can opt for a transfer to your bank account or a refund to My Credit in your account. If you’re a guest user, you will receive a Return Voucher.
